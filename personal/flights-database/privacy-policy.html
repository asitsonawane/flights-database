Privacy Policy

Flights Database Chrome Extension

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Flights Database Chrome Extension ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Important: This extension is designed to provide flight information and aircraft data. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 No Personal Data Collection

Our Chrome extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

2.2 Flight Data Only

The extension may access flight codes and aircraft information that you input or view, but this data:

  • Is processed locally within the extension
  • Is not stored permanently
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to use. The extension operates by:

  • Processing flight codes locally within your browser
  • Matching codes against our aircraft database
  • Displaying relevant aircraft information
  • All processing occurs within the extension's sandboxed environment

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

The extension may use Chrome's local storage API to:

  • Cache aircraft data for faster performance
  • Store user preferences (if any)
  • Maintain session information

All such data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security practices:

  • No external data transmission
  • Local processing only
  • Chrome's built-in security sandboxing
  • Regular security updates

5. Third-Party Services

Our extension does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services. We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media plugins
  • External tracking tools

6. Chrome Extension Permissions

Our extension requests the following permissions:

  • Active Tab: To read flight information from web pages
  • Storage: To cache data locally for better performance
  • Scripts: To inject content scripts for data extraction

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and do not enable any data collection or transmission.

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect personal data, this is not applicable to our service.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the policy in the Chrome Web Store
  • Including a notice in the extension's update notes
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date below

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, traditional data subject rights are not applicable. However, you have the right to:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear any locally stored data through Chrome's extension settings
  • Contact us with questions about this policy
  • Request information about our data practices

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@flightsdatabase.com
  • Extension Support: support@flightsdatabase.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

10. Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy is provided in compliance with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
